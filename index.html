About Me

Hello, I'm Harry Bui, a self-taught Data Analyst driven by my passion for harnessing the power of data. Despite my engineering background, I've developed a passion for data analysis, cultivating it through my research at The University of Melbourne and multiple certifications. I've worked on challenging data mining projects, applied machine learning techniques, and enhanced safety and efficiency in the construction industry. My ability to self-learn and adapt has led me to acquire advanced data skills, such as Python, SQL, and machine learning libraries. Now, I'm eager to transition into the Data Analyst/Data Scientist path, equipped with a firm commitment to leverage my expertise for meaningful business insights and data-driven solutions.
